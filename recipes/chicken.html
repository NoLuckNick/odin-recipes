Crockpot Chicken Recipe

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2lb chicken breast
  • Chicken Broth
  • Garlic Seasoning
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cooking Spray

For this recipe spray down crockpot with cooking spray then add chicken breast. Next season the chicken with seasonings above or add your own. After that pour the broth over the chicken (I like to cover the chicken). Cook until tender and chicken is 165 degrees.
