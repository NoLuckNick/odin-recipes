Crockpot Chicken Recipe

I love a nice easy meal (and by extension my cockpot). Use this recipe for an easy crockpot chicken recipe on a busy weeknight!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2lb chicken breast
  • Chicken Broth
  • Garlic Seasoning
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cooking Spray

Steps:

  1. Use cooking spray to lubricate the crockpot
  2. Add chicken to crockpot and season
  3. Add broth to crockpot. I usually like to cover the chicken.
  4. Cook on low for around 6 hours (until internal temp of 165 degrees) and enjoy
