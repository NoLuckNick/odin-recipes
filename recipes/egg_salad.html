Egg Salad

Maybe this is my inner golfer showing but I love a nice egg salad sandwich on a summer day! I find it goes great with plain potato chips.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 Dozen Eggs
  • Mayo
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Onions
  • Choice of Bread

STEPS:

  1. Make hard boiled eggs by boiling eggs for 12 minutes then peeling once cooled.
  2. Combine 1/2 cup mayo and 1/4 cup each mustard to bowl with eggs
  3. Chop and add onions
  4. Use a mixer to combine ingredients in a bowl and chill until ready to serve
  5. Make sandwich with your bread of choice. I prefer just white bread slices hit the best with egg salad
